📖 Important Formulas

Perimeter of Rectangle

= 2(l + b)

Area of Rectangle

= l × b

Area of Square

= a²

Area of Circle

= πr²

Circumference of Circle

= 2πr

Volume of Cube

= a³

Volume of Cuboid

= l × b × h

Volume of Cylinder

= πr²h

✍ Example

Find the area of a rectangle.

Length = 12 cm
Breadth = 8 cm

Area = 12 × 8 = 96 cm²

Find the volume of a cube.

Side = 5 cm

Volume = 5³ = 125 cm³
