📖 Important Formulas

(a+b)²

= a² + 2ab + b²

(a−b)²

= a² − 2ab + b²

(a+b)(a−b)

= a² − b²

x = (-b ± √(b²−4ac)) / 2a

✍ Example

Simplify:

(x+2)(x+5)

= x² +5x +2x +10

= x² +7x +10
