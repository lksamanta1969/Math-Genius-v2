📖 Important Formulas

Triangle Angle Sum

∠A + ∠B + ∠C = 180°

Area of Triangle

= ½ × Base × Height

Circumference of Circle

= 2πr

Area of Circle

= πr²

Pythagoras Theorem

c² = a² + b²

✍ Example

Find the area of a triangle.

Base = 10 cm
Height = 8 cm

Area = ½ × 10 × 8 = 40 cm²
