📖 Important Formulas

Probability

P(E) = Favorable Outcomes / Total Outcomes

Impossible Event

P(E) = 0

Certain Event

P(E) = 1

Complement Rule

P(E') = 1 − P(E)

Total Probability

0 ≤ P(E) ≤ 1

✍ Example

Rolling a Dice

Find the probability of getting 4.

Favourable Outcomes = 1

Total Outcomes = 6

P(4) = 1/6

Tossing a Coin

Probability of getting Head

P(H) = 1/2
