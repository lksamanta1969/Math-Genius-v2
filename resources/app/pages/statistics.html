📖 Important Formulas

Mean

= Sum of Observations / Number of Observations

Median

= Middle value of arranged data

Mode

= Most frequently occurring value

Range

= Highest Value − Lowest Value

Average

= Total ÷ Number of Items

✍ Example

Find the Mean

Data = 10, 15, 20, 25, 30

Mean = (10 + 15 + 20 + 25 + 30) / 5

= 100 / 5 = 20

Find the Range

Highest = 30
Lowest = 10

Range = 30 − 10 = 20
