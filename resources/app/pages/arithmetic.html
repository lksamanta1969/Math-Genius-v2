📖 Important Formulas

Percentage

Percentage = (Value / Total) × 100

Profit

Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price

Loss

Loss = Cost Price − Selling Price

Simple Interest

SI = (P × R × T) / 100

✍ Example

Find 25% of 480.

25 ÷ 100 × 480 = 120
